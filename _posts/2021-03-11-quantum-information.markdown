## Starting MIT's Online Course on Quantum Information
I've been wanting to read more about Quantum Information and Computation for a long while now, and I've tried reading the book for it couple of times. But I'm not the kind who can sit down with a book for hours studying something. So I started looking for a course online on this matter. To be honest I've been very lazy about it till now and haven't really looked for alternative resources on the subject.

Sometimes though, a particular professor catches your attention and manages to get you all fired up about the subject that he's teaching. That happened today. I started watching the MIT lecture series on this particular topic[^1]. I love the passion that Peter Shor teaches with. So I'm going to give this course a shot. Granted, I have a lot on my plate currently, with my ongoing project[^2], and the stupid coursework that I have to do as part of my degree, but I think I can make it work. It is something I'm finally really interested in, so I hope this drive lasts till I make some significant progress.

Well that's it for now. Unlike the thousands of things that I've started and left unfinished at some point, I hope I can actually get somewhere with this course. Wish me luck!
