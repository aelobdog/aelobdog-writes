# Semester over ?
Phew. That was one *hectic* semester. I'm glad that it is coming to an end. No more burdensome assignments, no more weekly or daily tests for three whole months. While the situation in my country is not getting any better, this is like a small bit of happiness. Here are a few more details on exactly why my third year of college has ended a month early.

India is not in a very good position right now on the whole, and the city I live in is experiencing a rise in Covid-19 cases so rapidly, it is like a rocket leaving the atmosphere at escape velocity. Covid-19 patients are no longer "acquaintances of acquaintances" but rather friends and family. In this dire situation, my university has chosen to let students take a "CLEAR" grade in as many courses as they want. This is a really great option to all those students who have experienced far too much stress during the semester. Personally, I'm going to choose to "CLEAR" all my subjects rather than writing the end semester exam. From my experience this semester, with all the unfair means being used to gain an advantage in courses, there is really no point in writing the final exams. At this point "exams" are nothing more than a game of who can cheat without the instructors knowing. And this is a sheer mockery of learning and education as a whole. Therefore, seeing as I have been under tremendous pressure due to academic requirements, and since exams have now become a joke, I'm choosing to not write them.

This has many upsides too! I can finally work on my 8086 emulator, I can finally study quantum information and computing, and also try my hand at music production as I've always wanted. I can also focus on actually "learning" stuff rather than "studying stuff to clear the exam". No offense to any of my professors, but I genuinely feel that I've learned nothing this semester. So now that it is in my hands, I'll try my best to learn as much as I can.

Anyways... I'll get back to whatever I was doing before going on this rant xD. Bye !
