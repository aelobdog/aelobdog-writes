## Cuppu ? What's that ?

Cuppu is a project I started to get to know how microprocessors work. I'm in my 3rd year of college, and as a part of my coursework, I have to take a course on Microprocessors and Interfacing[^1]. Since the course felt too dry and just learning the syntax of assembly language is not that fun, I have taken it upon myself to try and make an 8086 simulator/emulator to try and study by myself. With the help of my friend [samarthkm][1], I hope to complete this project latest by the start of the next semester.



## So what's happening *right now* ?

So far, we've added support for move instructions (with *all* of its damn addressing modes), push, and an incomplete implementation of the increment/decrement operations. Progress has been very, very slow for the past month or so since we began, but I hope we can speed up a little bit.
