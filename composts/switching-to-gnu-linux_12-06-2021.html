Switching to Gnu+Linux

12-06-2021


After a very, very long time... I've finally switched to gnu+linux full time Since I have a laptop that runs an Nvidia 1050Ti, I've switched to PopOS to escape the hell that is Nvidia driver management). It's a very liberating feeling, especially since I've been wanting to do this forever. No more Windows, no mode WSL (it's not bad, but it does feel a little clunky), no more mingw nonsense or even worse, MSVC and Visual Studio nonsense No more weird issues with no fixes online.

Now I'm finally in the land of fixable problems xD, coz don't get me wrong, Ubuntu as its own share of weird issues. But since the community has been good so far, I don't anticipate unfixable problems in the near future. Looking forward to this new life xD
